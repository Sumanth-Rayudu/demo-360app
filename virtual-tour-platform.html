What you get

Every tool to sell a property, beautifully.

🌐

360° Scene Viewer

Drop equirectangular JPEGs into a folder. They become fully interactive panoramic rooms instantly — no configuration required.

📍

Clickable Hotspots

Place hotspots anywhere in a scene. Info hotspots show tooltips — room dimensions, materials, USPs. Scene hotspots teleport buyers to the next room.

🔗

Shareable Links

Every tour lives at a clean URL. Share it on WhatsApp, email, or embed it on any website. Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

🎛️

Total Admin Control

Suspend a venture, disable a project, or toggle individual scenes — all from your dashboard, all reflected instantly with no redeployment.

🏷️

White-label & Custom Domains

Point client1.com to your server. Their buyers see their branding. You stay invisible — or visible, your choice. Full brand control per venture.

📈

SEO Ready

Every tour page is server-rendered. Google can crawl and index it. Property address, description, and images show as rich results in search.

Process

From drone shot to live tour in minutes.

01
📸

Upload JPEGs

Drop your equirectangular drone shots into the project folder. The system detects them automatically.

02
📍

Place Hotspots

Use the visual editor to click and place hotspots anywhere in the scene. Set type, label, and target.

03
🔗

Share the Link

Send the venture their tour URL. They share it with buyers. Works on any device, no app needed.

04
🎛️

Stay in Control

Suspend, enable, update, or disable any tour from your admin panel. Payment lapsed? One click to pause.
